Brian is an Assistant Professor of Instruction in the Computer Science Department at the University of Texas at Dallas. He received his PhD, MS, and BS degrees from UTD specializing in networking. Brian was co-advised by Bhavani Thuraisingham, Sriraam Natarajan, and Patrick Tague. He is also an active member of the MEWS research group at CMU-SV with Patrick Tague. His research interests lie in wireless networking and security, simulation of networks, and applications of Bayesian networks. For fun, Brian likes video game design, car audio, working out, and free food.
